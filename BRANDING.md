### Clear space

Always maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height and width of the "C" in CODEZEN. No text, graphics, or other elements should enter this zone.

### Do's

- Always use the official logo files from this repository
- Resize proportionally — never stretch or distort
- Use full-color version on solid, contrasting backgrounds
- Use monochrome (white) versions over photos or patterns
- Place the logo prominently so it is easily visible
- Include the logo in email signatures and official correspondence

### Don'ts

- Do not alter the colors of the logo
- Do not rotate, skew, or distort the logo
- Do not rearrange the mark and wordmark
- Do not place the full-color logo on a busy photo or pattern (use the white version instead)
- Do not add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
- Do not use the logo at sizes where the mark becomes illegible
- Do not change the font of the wordmark
- Do not place text inside the clear space zone

- Body text on dark backgrounds: White. Body text on light backgrounds: gray 900 (`#212529`)
- Do not introduce colors outside this palette without brand approval

## Typography

### Fonts

| Font | Role | Weights used |
|---|---|---|
| Felix Titling | Logo wordmark only | Regular (caps only) |
| Outfit | All other text | Regular, Semibold, Bold |

Font files are in `fonts/`.

**Felix Titling is exclusive to the logo wordmark. It must not be used for body copy, headings, or any other text.**

### Type hierarchy

| Level | Font | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Headline | Outfit | Bold | Section titles, hero text |
| Subheading | Outfit | Semibold | Sub-sections, card titles |
| Body | Outfit | Regular | Paragraphs, descriptions |

### Digital guidelines

- Web font: Outfit (load via the files in `fonts/outfit/` or Google Fonts)
- Fallback stack: `'Outfit', Arial, sans-serif`
- Minimum body size: 12pt / 16px for legibility
- Line height: 1.5× the font size
- Do not substitute fonts without brand approval

### Icon usage

Icons are designed as line art on a **dark background**. Always place them on a dark container (Night `#212529` or Dark Blue `#322A84`). On light backgrounds, the icons will lose definition and appear weak — do not use them this way without a backing element.

Recommended container: dark rounded rectangle or square, icon centered with ~20% padding.

## Imagery

Photography and 3D renders should follow this style:

- **Background:** Deep dark (near black or Night `#212529`)
- **Subject:** Translucent, glass, or crystalline geometric forms — prisms, cubes, polyhedra
- **Color accent:** Cool iridescent blues, purples, and whites catching light through the forms
- **Mood:** Precise, technical, architectural — not warm, organic, or illustrative
- **Avoid:** Stock photography of people, generic "hacker in a dark room" clichés, warm/orange tones
